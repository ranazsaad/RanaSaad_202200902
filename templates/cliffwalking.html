How It Works
  • Agent starts at S (bottom-left) and must reach G (bottom-right)
  • Agent receives +10.0 reward for reaching goal
  • Falling off cliff gives -100.0 reward and resets to start
  • Each safe step costs -1.0 reward
  • The optimal path is to walk just above the cliff to minimize risk
Algorithms
  • Q-Learning: Off-policy, model-free
  • SARSA: On-policy, model-free
  • Monte Carlo: Model-free, learns from complete episodes
  • TD Learning: Temporal Difference learning
  • N-step TD: Multi-step temporal difference
